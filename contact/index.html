Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download our app and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the simple registration process to create your account.

How do I withdraw my winnings?

Go to your wallet section, click on "Withdraw", enter the amount, and select your preferred payment method.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit cards, and popular e-wallets.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information.
