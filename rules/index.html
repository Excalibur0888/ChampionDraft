Game Rules

Master the game with our comprehensive rules and guidelines. Understanding these rules is crucial for fair play and an enjoyable gaming experience.

General Overview

Basic Rules

  • Create your team within the given credit limit
  • Select players from both teams participating in the match
  • Choose a captain and vice-captain for bonus points
  • Submit your team before the deadline
  • Follow all fair play guidelines
  • Verify your account before participating
  • Keep your account details secure

Team Creation

  • 100 credit points available for team creation
  • Select 11 players for cricket matches
  • Maximum 7 players allowed from one team
  • Mandatory position requirements must be met
  • Captain scores 2x points, Vice-captain 1.5x points
  • Balance your team across all positions
  • Consider player form and conditions

Fair Play

  • One user can create only one team per match
  • No use of multiple accounts allowed
  • No sharing of teams before match starts
  • Violation may lead to account suspension
  • Regular fair play monitoring in place
  • Report suspicious activity
  • Maintain sporting spirit

Scoring System

Cricket Scoring

Batting Points

  • Run Scored: 1 point
  • Boundary Bonus: 1 point
  • Six Bonus: 2 points
  • Half Century: 20 points
  • Century: 40 points
  • Duck: -2 points
  • 30+ Run Bonus: 4 points

Bowling Points

  • Wicket: 25 points
  • 4+ Wickets: 20 bonus
  • 5+ Wickets: 40 bonus
  • Maiden Over: 12 points
  • Economy Rate Bonus: Up to 6 points
  • 3+ Wickets: 10 bonus
  • LBW/Bowled: 2 bonus

Fielding Points

  • Catch: 8 points
  • Stumping: 12 points
  • Run Out: 12 points
  • Direct Hit Bonus: 4 points
  • 3+ Catches: 8 bonus
  • Catch & Run Out: 16 points
  • Quick Stumping: 4 bonus
